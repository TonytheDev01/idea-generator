How It Works

From zero ideas to 30 posts
in 3 practical steps

No learning curve. No complex setup. Just describe what you do and let the engine do the heavy lifting.

1

Describe Your Brand

Tell Idea-Engne about your niche, your audience, and your brand personality. The more specific, the sharper the ideas.

Example: "I'm a fitness coach for busy moms who want to lose weight without going to the gym."
2

Generate Instantly

Hit generate and get 30 AI-crafted post ideas in under 60 seconds. Each idea includes a hook, format suggestion, and hashtag set.

Output includes: Post hook, content angle, platform fit, and suggested CTA.
3

Save, Schedule & Ship

Save your favourites to your personal idea vault, export to your calendar, and start posting with confidence. Never miss a day.

Export to: Notion, Google Sheets, Calendar, or plain text.
